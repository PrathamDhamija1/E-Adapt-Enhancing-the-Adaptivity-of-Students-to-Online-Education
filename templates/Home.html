Why Choose e-adapt?

Real-Time Tracking

Integrates questionnaire and LMS data to dynamically monitor student engagement and adaptivity in online learning environments.

Personalized Interventions

Uses Reinforcement Learning to provide tailored support plans, such as resource recommendations and time management workshops.

Gamified Motivation

Awards badges (Bronze, Silver, Gold) to boost student engagement, driven by neural network-based gamification.

Powerful Ensemble Model

Combines Gradient Boosting, SVM, and TCN for accurate adaptivity predictions (>92% accuracy).

Explainable Insights

Provides transparent predictions using SHAP and LIME, helping educators understand key factors like internet reliability.

User-Friendly Interface

Accessible web platform for students and educators to predict adaptivity and access personalized support.
